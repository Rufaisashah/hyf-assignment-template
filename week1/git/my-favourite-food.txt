Step 2: Cook the Rice

Step 3: Fry the Onions

Heat ghee/oil in a large heavy-bottomed pan.

Step 4: Cook the Chicken

In the same pan with remaining onions, add the marinated chicken.

Cook until chicken is almost done and oil starts separating.

Add chopped tomato and cook for 5 minutes.

Step 5: Layer the Biryani

Remove half of the chicken masala from the pan.

Layer half of the cooked rice on top of the remaining chicken.

Add mint, coriander, fried onions, and saffron milk.

Add the remaining chicken and rice on top and repeat the garnish.
