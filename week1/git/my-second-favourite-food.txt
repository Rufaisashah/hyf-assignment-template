🧄 Ingredients

500g chicken (boneless, bite-sized pieces)

1 tbsp oil or butter

4 garlic cloves (minced)

3 tbsp honey

2 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice

Salt & pepper to taste

Optional: chili flakes or spring onions for garnish

🍳 Instructions

Season chicken with salt and pepper.

Cook chicken in a pan with oil until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.

In the same pan, sauté garlic until fragrant.

Add honey, soy sauce, and vinegar, stir well.

Return chicken to the pan, toss to coat evenly.

Cook for 2–3 minutes until sauce thickens and glazes the chicken.

✨ Serve with: rice, noodles, or steamed veggies.
